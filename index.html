About Nausicaa

Regional expertise that isn't otherwise on your doorstep.

Nausicaa Consult is led by Callum Rideout, a Broome-based Principal Consultant with an MBA majoring in Finance and a career spanning oil & gas, underground and open-pit mining, bulk earthworks, and government civil construction.

That operational grounding is paired with direct experience supporting Western Australian Government consultation and policy delivery, including work with Ministry of Data on WA Government programs.

The result is a consultant who understands both the boardroom and the bulldozer — and who can translate that into practical systems, tender-ready documentation, and growth pathways for businesses across Northern WA.

  • Oil & gas operations
  • UG & open-pit mining
  • Bulk earthworks & civil construction
